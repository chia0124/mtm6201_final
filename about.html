About

History

Founded in 2018, Pawsitive Futures was born from a deep love for animals and a desire to make a lasting impact in our community. From the moment we opened our doors, our goal has been simple: to provide a safe and loving environment for animals who have faced hardship and uncertainty. We work with local shelters, foster families, and volunteers to ensure every animal receives the care and attention they need to find their forever homes.

Our Services

  • Animal Rescue: We take in animals from all walks of life, providing shelter, care, and rehabilitation.
  • Adoption Services: Matching animals with families is our priority.
  • Foster Program: Our foster families play a vital role in giving animals the care they need until they find their forever homes.
  • Education & Outreach: We aim to raise awareness about animal welfare and responsible pet ownership.
